Collaboration Interests

I welcome collaborations in the following areas:

  • Dynamic constitutive modeling — new material systems, validation campaigns, FEM implementation
  • Ballistic impact — experimental programs, protective structure design, AI-driven prediction models
  • Battery impact safety — mechanical-electrochemical characterization, AE monitoring, PHM algorithms
  • Multiphysics simulation — coupled mechanical-thermal-electrochemical FEM modeling

I am open to both short-term visiting researcher arrangements and long-term joint research programs. If your work intersects with any of these areas, please reach out.

Prospective Graduate Students

If you are a highly motivated student interested in pursuing a PhD or postdoctoral position in impact dynamics, constitutive modeling, or battery safety, please send me an email with:

  • A brief statement of your research interests and goals
  • Your CV / resume
  • Representative publications or thesis (if applicable)

Note: Positions are subject to availability and institutional funding. Students with strong backgrounds in solid mechanics, materials science, or applied machine learning are particularly encouraged to apply.
